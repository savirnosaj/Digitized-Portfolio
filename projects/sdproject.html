Sportsday Thursday(SDT) -

is sport team managing app used to make creating teams beforehand of event.

Language(s): C#, HTML, CSS, JS

Framework(s): ASP.NET, Entity

Summary: When I was a student at the coding dojo, we attended a sportsday that was held every Thursday. The number of people attending kept decreasing every week. I wanted to create an app that would inspire people to go and so the app I built allowed people to create, join teams, and find upcoming sporting events.

Functionality: You login/register, and will be redirected to all upcoming sport events. Choose to join a game, or create an event for yourself.
